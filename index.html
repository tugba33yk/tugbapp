Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Tugbapp ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our Etsy shop management application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information from Etsy API

When you connect your Etsy shop to Tugbapp, we collect and process the following data through the Etsy API:

  • Shop information (shop name, shop ID, location)
  • Listing data (titles, descriptions, prices, quantities, images, tags)
  • Order information (order details, customer information, shipping addresses)
  • Transaction history and sales data
  • Inventory and stock levels

1.2 Account Information

  • Email address
  • Name (if provided)
  • Authentication tokens for Etsy API access

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Shop Management: To provide inventory management, listing optimization, and order tracking features
  • Analytics: To generate sales reports and performance insights for your shop
  • Notifications: To send you alerts about orders, inventory levels, and important updates
  • Service Improvement: To improve our application features and user experience
  • Support: To respond to your inquiries and provide customer support

3. Data Security

We take data security seriously and implement appropriate measures to protect your information:

  • All data is encrypted in transit using SSL/TLS protocols
  • Data at rest is encrypted using industry-standard encryption methods
  • Access to your data is restricted to authorized personnel only
  • We use secure servers and regularly update our security measures
